Rules regarding airline names

  1. Name of your airline cannot contain names of real world airlines, or any other trademarks.
  2. The airline name may not be too similar to any real world airline (for example EazyJet is not approved).
  3. Name must be appropriate for an airline and may not be offending
  4. You are not allowed to use real world airline logos in the airline livery or logo.
  5. Administration has the right to remove airlines not following these rules.

Tips for selecting the home base

  1. All your flights must depart from your home airport.
  2. Make sure the airport is large enough and has enough passengers.
  3. Check also that there are no other (or only few) airlines based at the same airport.
  4. Also check that the airport has enough free airport slots available.
